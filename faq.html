FAQ for Transporation Employees


Frequently Asked Questions

Updated: Friday May 22, 2020

1. With summer instruction offered virtually, will I be paid if I accepted a summer work assignment?


There are limited summer assignments that will be completed such as bus maintenance. Drivers who have signed up for this summer task and drivers who are assigned to support the food delivery service will be paid for all hours worked. In order to be paid for summer work, the work must be assigned and completed.

2. If we are not paid during the canceled summer work assignment, will I be able to collect unemployment benefits during the summer?


FCPS cannot advise you on your eligibility for unemployment benefits. Eligibility for unemployment benefits is solely a function of the Virginia Employment Commission. You may visit the Virginia Employment Commission website for further assistance with your inquiry.

3. When will we be asked to conduct summer clean out and remove personal items from and start my bus?


A decision has not been made on this timing. If you have a necessity to remove a personal item, please contact your immediate supervisor for guidance.

5. Can I work another job while getting paid by FCPS?


You should not be employed by anyone other than FCPS during the hours that you are normally working for FCPS while you are still being paid (employed) by FCPS.
