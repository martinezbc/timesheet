EQUIPMENT/LIFT/ADMIN: In order to enable the equipment/lift button (Q/L) or admin button (J), you must enter in your route number as it is printed on the route sheet.

OTHER WORK DUTY: To add an other work duty, click on Add Other Work and complete the section. To remove the other work duty, click the trash can in the corner.

FIELD TRIP: To add a field trip, click on Add Field Trip and complete all fields. To remove the field trip, click the trash can in the corner.

LEAVE: To record leave taken, click on Add Leave. Leave type must be selected.
Enter time for partial leave or click ALL DAY if leave was taken for the entire day. Click Remove Leave to clear leave for that day.
